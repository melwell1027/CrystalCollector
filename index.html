Crystal Collector

  • You will be given a target number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your number.
  • You win the game by matching your number to the target number. You lose the game if your number goes above the target number.
  • The value of each crystal changes each game and is hidden from you until you click on it.
